Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Echo respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the Echo desktop application and related services.

Information we process

Echo may process account connection data, calendar metadata, conversation content, and application settings solely to provide requested application features.

Google Calendar data

If you connect Google Calendar, Echo accesses calendar information only after you explicitly authorize the connection. Calendar data is used only to display, sync, and support calendar-related features within the application.

Data sharing

Echo does not sell your personal information. Data is only shared with service providers or APIs that are necessary to deliver the features you request.

Security

Echo uses reasonable safeguards to protect stored tokens, local settings, and connected account data. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your choices

You can disconnect your connected calendar account at any time within the app. You may also stop using the application whenever you choose.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, update this section with your support email before publishing.
